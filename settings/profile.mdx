Your **profile** stores the identity other users see in comments, run history, and audit logs. Keeping it accurate speeds up collaboration and helps security teams map actions to real people.

## Personal information

Update:

- **Display name** – shown in UI and notifications
- **Email** – primary login identifier where your workspace uses email-based auth
- **Avatar** – optional image from your SSO provider or uploaded file (policy-dependent)

Some fields sync from your **identity provider** when SSO is enforced; inline edits may be disabled—request changes through IT if values are wrong.

## Preferences

Depending on deployment, you may set:

- **Default workspace** or landing page after login
- **Locale** and **time display** formats for schedules you read in the UI
- **Editor** preferences for pipeline JSON/YAML when applicable

<Tip>
  Align your profile time zone with where you interpret cron schedules most often; pipeline time zones are still set per schedule.
</Tip>

## Security quick links

From the profile area you can jump to:

- [Multi-factor authentication](/settings/mfa)
- [API keys](/settings/api-keys) you own
- Active **sessions** (sign out other devices) when supported

<Info>
  If your email changes, verify re-invitations to shared resources and update any external runbooks that reference your old address.
</Info>
